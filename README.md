# Cofounder+
Available on the appstore https://apps.apple.com/us/app/cofounder/id6753924216

Cofounder+ is an AI-driven system designed to help individuals and small teams operate like structured businesses.

Most people don’t fail because they lack ideas. They fail because they don’t know what to do next, how to prioritize, or how to execute consistently.

Cofounder+ solves that by translating goals, workflows, and real-world constraints into clear, actionable systems across core business functions.

---

## What It Does

Cofounder+ acts as a digital cofounder that bridges strategy and execution.

It helps users:

* Break down business goals into structured, executable tasks
* Generate and prioritize product, marketing, sales, and operational workflows
* Track progress across multiple areas of a business in one system
* Automate repetitive decisions and actions using AI

---

## Core Areas

The platform is structured around the core functions of a business:

* Product — feature planning, development workflows, execution tracking
* Marketing — campaigns, content, outreach systems
* Sales — leads, deals, customer management
* Finance — transactions, budgeting, projections
* Operations — tasks, workflows, and system organization

---

## How It Works

Cofounder+ focuses on execution, not just insight.

Instead of giving abstract advice, it:

1. Understands the user’s business context
2. Generates structured workflows and tasks
3. Prioritizes what should be done next
4. Tracks execution and progress
5. Continuously adapts based on results

---

## Technical Overview

* Frontend: React + TypeScript
* Backend: Supabase
* AI: OpenAI / Claude integrations
* Mobile: iOS (Capacitor)

The system is designed to support real-time data updates, automation, and cross-functional workflows across a single platform.

---

## Why This Exists

Running a business requires managing dozens of moving parts across different systems.

Most tools solve isolated problems. Cofounder+ is designed to unify them into a single operating layer that helps users think, decide, and execute more effectively.
